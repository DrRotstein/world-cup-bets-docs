## Context

This is the core interaction. User sees upcoming matches, picks a score (e.g. Brazil 2 - Germany 1), and submits. Once the match kicks off, the bet is locked. Points are calculated after the match ends (handled by S-05).

## Scope

- Bet table: id, userId, groupId, matchId, homeScorePrediction, awayScorePrediction, createdAt, updatedAt
- POST /groups/:groupId/bets — place or update a bet
- GET /groups/:groupId/bets — list my bets in this group
- GET /groups/:groupId/matches/:matchId/bets — all bets for a match (visible only after kickoff to prevent copying)
- Validation: reject bets after kickoff time
- Frontend: match list with score input, submit button, confirmation

## Non-goals

- No bet on "winner of tournament" or "top scorer" — just match scores
- No partial bets (must predict both home and away score)
- No bet history page for MVP (just current/upcoming)
