## Context

Risk R-1 says friends won't sign up if onboarding is heavy. Google OAuth is one tap — minimal friction. No passwords to remember, no verification emails to wait for.

## Scope

- Google OAuth 2.0 flow (backend handles token exchange)
- JWT-based session (stored in httpOnly cookie or Authorization header)
- User table: id, email, displayName, avatarUrl, createdAt
- Frontend: "Sign in with Google" button on landing page
- Redirect back to app after auth

## Non-goals

- No other OAuth providers (Apple, GitHub, etc.) for MVP
- No email/password fallback
- No role-based permissions (all users are equal)
